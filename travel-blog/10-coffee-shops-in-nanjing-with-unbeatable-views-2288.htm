10 Coffee Shops in Nanjing with Unbeatable Views

  • Home
  • Travel Blog
  • Blog Details

Nanjing, a city steeped in history and natural beauty, offers some of the most picturesque coffee shops in China. Whether you're a traveler seeking a quiet spot to unwind or a local looking for inspiration, these cafés combine great brews with breathtaking views. Here are 10 coffee shops in Nanjing where the scenery is as unforgettable as the coffee.

1. Xuanwu Lake Perk

Lakeside Serenity

Nestled along the shores of Xuanwu Lake, this café offers panoramic views of the water and the surrounding greenery. The floor-to-ceiling windows ensure you don’t miss a single ripple on the lake’s surface.

Must-Try Drink

Their signature Lavender Latte pairs perfectly with the tranquil atmosphere. Sip slowly while watching boats glide across the water.

2. Zijin Mountain Roasters

Elevated Coffee with a View

Perched on the slopes of Zijin Mountain, this spot provides a stunning overlook of Nanjing’s skyline. The outdoor terrace is ideal for sunrise or sunset coffee sessions.

Specialty Brew

Try their Mountain Mist Espresso, a bold shot with a hint of citrus, best enjoyed with the cool mountain breeze.

3. Qinhuai River Brew Bar

Historic Waterfront Vibes

Located along the Qinhuai River, this café blends modern aesthetics with traditional architecture. Watch lantern-lit boats pass by as you enjoy your drink.

Local Favorite

The Osmanthus Cold Brew is a refreshing nod to Nanjing’s floral heritage.

4. Sun Yat-sen Mansion Café

A Blend of History and Scenery

Adjacent to the Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum, this café offers views of lush gardens and historic landmarks. The colonial-style building adds to the charm.

Top Pick

Their Nanjing Fog (a matcha-infused latte) is a must for green tea lovers.

5. Confucius Temple Coffee Loft

Cultural Sips

Overlooking the bustling Confucius Temple area, this loft-style café provides a bird’s-eye view of the lively streets below.

Unique Offering

The Red Bean Mocha combines local flavors with classic coffee for a truly Nanjing experience.

6. Nanjing Eye Overlook

Modern Skyline Views

Situated near the Nanjing Eye pedestrian bridge, this café boasts floor-to-ceiling windows framing the Yangtze River and the city’s modern architecture.

Drink Recommendation

The Skyline Cappuccino comes with latte art inspired by the bridge’s design.

7. Ming Palace Grounds Café

Ruins and Relaxation

Set within the Ming Xiaoling Mausoleum complex, this café lets you sip coffee amid ancient stone sculptures and towering trees.

Signature Sip

The Emperor’s Blend is a dark roast with subtle spice notes, fit for royalty.

8. Purple Peak Panorama

Hilltop Hideaway

Tucked away in the Purple Mountain area, this café offers sweeping views of forests and distant pagodas. The quiet ambiance makes it perfect for reading or reflection.

Must-Order

Their Wild Honey Flat White is sweetened with local honey for a natural touch.

9. Nanjing Yangtze River Deck

Riverside Retreat

With an open-air deck overlooking the Yangtze, this spot is ideal for watching ships sail by. The industrial-chic décor complements the waterfront vibe.

Best Brew

The Riverboat Cold Brew is smooth and slightly smoky, just like the fog over the water at dawn.

10. Old City Wall Nook

Timeless Views

Built into a restored section of Nanjing’s ancient city wall, this café offers a unique vantage point of old and new Nanjing.

Specialty Drink

The Stone Wall Mocha is rich and earthy, mirroring the café’s historic surroundings.

Each of these coffee shops offers more than just a caffeine fix—they provide unforgettable backdrops that enhance every sip. Whether you're drawn to lakes, mountains, or historic sites, Nanjing’s café scene has a view (and a brew) for you.
