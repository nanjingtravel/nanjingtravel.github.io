10 Expat-Friendly Vegan & Vegetarian Restaurants in Nanjing

  • Home
  • Travel Blog
  • Blog Details

Nanjing, a city steeped in history and culture, is also a hidden gem for plant-based food lovers. Whether you're a long-term expat or a traveler passing through, the city offers a surprising variety of vegan and vegetarian dining options. From cozy cafés to upscale eateries, here’s a curated list of 10 expat-friendly spots that cater to diverse tastes and dietary preferences.

1. Veggie Table (素食餐桌)

Why It’s a Must-Visit

Located in the heart of Xinjiekou, Veggie Table is a haven for health-conscious diners. The restaurant focuses on organic, locally sourced ingredients, offering a mix of Chinese and Western dishes.

What to Try

  • Mushroom "Pork" Baozi – A vegan twist on a Nanjing classic.
  • Avocado & Quinoa Salad – Perfect for a light, nutritious meal.

2. Loving Hut (爱心之家)

Global Vegan Chain with Local Flair

Part of an international vegan chain, Loving Hut in Nanjing serves affordable, flavorful dishes with a focus on sustainability.

Signature Dishes

  • Vegan "Beef" Noodles – A hearty, protein-rich option.
  • Sweet & Sour "Chicken" – Made with soy protein, it’s a crowd-pleaser.

3. Pure & Whole (清蔬)

Aesthetic Dining Experience

This stylish restaurant near Confucius Temple combines minimalist décor with creative plant-based cuisine.

Top Picks

  • Truffle Dumplings – Luxurious and aromatic.
  • Matcha Tiramisu – A guilt-free dessert.

4. Green Olive (绿橄榄)

Mediterranean-Inspired Vegan Fare

Green Olive stands out with its Middle Eastern flavors, offering hummus, falafel, and freshly baked flatbreads.

Must-Order

  • Falafel Wrap – Crispy on the outside, soft inside.
  • Rosewater Lemonade – A refreshing drink to complement your meal.

5. Vegetarian Lifestyle (蔬食生活)

Homestyle Cooking

A cozy spot near Zhongshan Ling, this restaurant specializes in comforting, homestyle vegetarian dishes.

Best Bites

  • Braised "Fish" with Tofu Skin – A convincing plant-based alternative.
  • Stir-Fried Seasonal Greens – Simple yet delicious.

6. The Veggie (素食主义)

Western-Friendly Menu

Popular among expats, The Veggie offers burgers, pasta, and even vegan cheese platters.

What’s Special

  • Beyond Burger – Juicy and satisfying.
  • Vegan Carbonara – Creamy without the dairy.

7. Tianxiang Vegetarian (天香斋)

Buddhist-Inspired Cuisine

This serene restaurant near Xuanwu Lake serves traditional Buddhist vegetarian meals, free of garlic and onions.

Try These

  • Lotus Root Soup – Light and nourishing.
  • Steamed Glutinous Rice Balls – A subtly sweet treat.

8. Sprout Lifestyle (芽米生活)

Healthy Café Vibes

A great spot for brunch, Sprout Lifestyle offers smoothie bowls, fresh juices, and wholesome baked goods.

Brunch Favorites

  • Acai Bowl – Packed with superfoods.
  • Avocado Toast – A classic done right.

9. Veggie Farm (蔬食农场)

Farm-to-Table Concept

With an emphasis on fresh, seasonal produce, Veggie Farm is ideal for those who appreciate simplicity and quality.

Recommended Dishes

  • Pumpkin Curry – Rich and aromatic.
  • Fresh Spring Rolls – Light and crunchy.

10. Happy Buddha (快乐佛)

Fusion Flavors

This quirky restaurant blends Asian and Western influences, offering everything from sushi to vegan pizza.

Don’t Miss

  • Vegan "Duck" Pancakes – A creative take on Peking duck.
  • Mango Sticky Rice – A sweet ending to your meal.

Nanjing’s vegan and vegetarian scene is thriving, and these 10 spots prove that plant-based dining can be diverse, delicious, and expat-friendly. Whether you're craving local flavors or international dishes, there’s something for every palate.
