Best Airbnb Stays in Nanjing for an Unforgettable Trip

  • Home
  • Travel Blog
  • Blog Details

Nanjing, the ancient capital of China, is a city where history and modernity collide. From the majestic Ming Xiaoling Mausoleum to the bustling Confucius Temple, every corner tells a story. But to truly immerse yourself in the charm of this city, you need the perfect place to stay. Airbnb offers a range of unique accommodations that can elevate your trip from ordinary to extraordinary. Whether you’re looking for a traditional courtyard home, a chic loft with skyline views, or a cozy riverside retreat, Nanjing has it all. Here’s a curated list of the best Airbnb stays in Nanjing for an unforgettable experience.

1. Luxury Penthouse with Qinhuai River Views

Why Stay Here?

If you want to wake up to breathtaking views of the Qinhuai River, this penthouse is a dream come true. Located in the heart of Nanjing, this modern loft combines sleek design with unbeatable convenience. Floor-to-ceiling windows let you soak in the cityscape, while the rooftop terrace is perfect for evening cocktails.

Highlights

  • Prime Location: Steps away from Confucius Temple and Laomendong.
  • Stylish Interiors: Minimalist decor with high-end amenities.
  • Rooftop Access: Private space for stargazing or morning yoga.

Nearby Attractions

  • Confucius Temple (5-minute walk)
  • Nanjing Museum (15-minute drive)
  • Zhonghua Gate (10-minute walk)

2. Traditional Siheyuan in the Old Town

Why Stay Here?

For a taste of Nanjing’s rich history, this restored siheyuan (courtyard house) is a hidden gem. With its wooden beams, antique furnishings, and tranquil courtyard, you’ll feel like you’ve stepped back in time.

Highlights

  • Authentic Charm: Original architecture with modern comforts.
  • Peaceful Escape: A quiet retreat from the city buzz.
  • Cultural Experience: Host offers calligraphy workshops.

Nearby Attractions

  • Presidential Palace (10-minute walk)
  • Nanjing Library (5-minute walk)
  • Xinjiekou Shopping District (15-minute walk)

3. Artist’s Loft Near Xuanwu Lake

Why Stay Here?

Creative souls will adore this loft, owned by a local artist. The space is filled with original paintings, handmade ceramics, and a cozy reading nook. Plus, it’s just a short stroll from Xuanwu Lake, one of Nanjing’s most scenic spots.

Highlights

  • Artsy Vibes: Unique decor that inspires creativity.
  • Lake Proximity: Perfect for morning jogs or sunset walks.
  • Host’s Guidance: Get insider tips on local art galleries.

Nearby Attractions

  • Xuanwu Lake Park (3-minute walk)
  • Jiming Temple (10-minute walk)
  • Nanjing Drum Tower (15-minute walk)

4. Boutique Treehouse in Purple Mountain

Why Stay Here?

Nestled in the lush greenery of Purple Mountain, this treehouse is a nature lover’s paradise. The wooden cabin-style design blends seamlessly with the surroundings, offering a serene escape without sacrificing comfort.

Highlights

  • Secluded Retreat: Surrounded by forests and hiking trails.
  • Eco-Friendly: Solar-powered and sustainable materials.
  • Stargazing Deck: Unobstructed views of the night sky.

Nearby Attractions

  • Ming Xiaoling Mausoleum (10-minute walk)
  • Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum (15-minute drive)
  • Zixia Lake (5-minute walk)

5. Modern Apartment Near Nanjing Eye

Why Stay Here?

This sleek apartment is ideal for travelers who love contemporary design. Located near the iconic Nanjing Eye pedestrian bridge, it offers easy access to riverside walks and vibrant nightlife.

Highlights

  • Designer Touches: Scandinavian-inspired interiors.
  • Smart Home Features: Voice-controlled lighting and AC.
  • Riverside Location: Perfect for evening strolls.

Nearby Attractions

  • Nanjing Eye (5-minute walk)
  • Jiangsu Art Museum (10-minute drive)
  • Hexi CBD (15-minute walk)

6. Cozy Riverside Cottage in Qixia District

Why Stay Here?

If you prefer a quieter side of Nanjing, this cottage by the Yangtze River is a peaceful haven. The rustic charm, combined with modern amenities, makes it perfect for couples or solo travelers seeking relaxation.

Highlights

  • Waterfront Setting: Private dock for morning coffee.
  • Homely Feel: Fireplace and handmade quilts.
  • Local Flavors: Host provides homemade tangbao (soup dumplings).

Nearby Attractions

  • Qixia Mountain (20-minute drive)
  • Yangtze River Bridge (15-minute drive)
  • Qixia Temple (10-minute walk)

7. Minimalist Studio in Xinjiekou

Why Stay Here?

Budget-conscious travelers will love this chic studio in Xinjiekou, Nanjing’s shopping and dining hub. Despite its compact size, the clever design maximizes space, making it both functional and stylish.

Highlights

  • Affordable Luxury: High-end finishes at a great price.
  • Central Location: Walking distance to malls and metro.
  • Compact Kitchenette: Ideal for light meals.

Nearby Attractions

  • Deji Plaza (5-minute walk)
  • Nanjing Fuzimiao (10-minute drive)
  • Hunan Road Food Street (15-minute walk)

8. Luxury Villa in Jiangning District

Why Stay Here?

For those who want to splurge, this villa offers privacy and opulence. With a private pool, landscaped garden, and panoramic mountain views, it’s perfect for group getaways or special occasions.

Highlights

  • Resort-Style Amenities: Pool, BBQ area, and home theater.
  • Spacious Layout: 4 bedrooms with ensuite bathrooms.
  • Scenic Surroundings: Close to Tangshan Hot Springs.

Nearby Attractions

  • Tangshan Hot Springs (10-minute drive)
  • Niushou Mountain (20-minute drive)
  • Jiangning University Town (15-minute drive)

9. Bohemian Rooftop Apartment in Gulou

Why Stay Here?

This eclectic rooftop apartment in Gulou is a feast for the senses. Vibrant tapestries, vintage furniture, and a rooftop garden create a whimsical atmosphere. Plus, it’s in one of Nanjing’s trendiest neighborhoods.

Highlights

  • Unique Decor: A mix of global and local artistry.
  • Rooftop Oasis: Perfect for morning coffee or evening wine.
  • Cultural Hub: Surrounded by indie cafes and live music bars.

Nearby Attractions

  • Gulou (Drum Tower) (5-minute walk)
  • Nanjing University (10-minute walk)
  • Shanghai Road (15-minute walk)

10. Floating House on Mochou Lake

Why Stay Here?

For a truly unique experience, book this floating house on Mochou Lake. The gentle rocking of the water and the serene views make it a one-of-a-kind stay.

Highlights

  • Waterfront Living: Direct access to the lake.
  • Romantic Ambiance: Ideal for couples.
  • Sunrise Views: Wake up to golden reflections on the water.

Nearby Attractions

  • Mochou Lake Park (on-site)
  • Nanjing Yunjin Museum (10-minute drive)
  • Hanzhongmen (15-minute walk)

No matter which Airbnb you choose, Nanjing promises a trip filled with culture, beauty, and unforgettable memories. Each of these stays offers something special, ensuring your visit to this historic city is nothing short of extraordinary.
