Best Bike Rental Services Near Nanjing Universities

  • Home
  • Travel Blog
  • Blog Details

Cycling is one of the best ways to explore Nanjing, especially for students and travelers looking for an affordable and eco-friendly mode of transportation. With its rich history, scenic parks, and vibrant university districts, Nanjing offers plenty of bike-friendly routes. If you're studying at or visiting one of Nanjing’s top universities, renting a bike can be a game-changer. Here’s a detailed guide to the best bike rental services near Nanjing’s major campuses.

Why Rent a Bike in Nanjing?

Nanjing is a cyclist-friendly city with well-maintained bike lanes, flat terrains in many areas, and stunning sights perfect for a leisurely ride. Whether you're commuting to class, exploring the city’s historical landmarks, or just enjoying a weekend ride along the Qinhuai River, biking is a fantastic option.

Benefits of Biking Near Universities

  • Cost-effective – Cheaper than taxis or ride-sharing apps.
  • Convenient – Avoid traffic jams and crowded public transport.
  • Eco-friendly – Reduce your carbon footprint while getting around.
  • Healthier – A great way to stay active between classes.

Top Bike Rental Services Near Nanjing Universities

1. HelloBike (哈啰单车) – Best for Short-Term Rentals

HelloBike is one of the most popular bike-sharing platforms in Nanjing. With its bright blue bikes scattered across the city, it’s incredibly easy to find one near universities like Nanjing University (NJU) and Southeast University (SEU).

Features:

  • App-based rental – Scan the QR code to unlock.
  • Affordable rates – Around ¥1.5 per 30 minutes.
  • Wide availability – Bikes are parked at designated spots.

Best Routes Nearby:

  • Xuanwu Lake Loop – A scenic 10km ride perfect for relaxation.
  • Nanjing University to Confucius Temple – A cultural ride through the city center.

2. Mobike (摩拜单车) – Reliable & Durable Bikes

Mobike is another excellent choice, known for its sturdy orange bikes. It’s widely available near Nanjing Normal University and Hohai University.

Features:

  • No fixed parking stations – Park anywhere within service zones.
  • Long battery life – Electric-assist options available.
  • User-friendly app – Real-time bike location tracking.

Best Routes Nearby:

  • Zhongshan Mountain – A challenging but rewarding ride for adventure seekers.
  • Qinhuai River Trail – A picturesque evening ride.

3. Didi Bike (滴滴青桔) – Great for Students

Didi Bike, operated by the ride-hailing giant Didi, offers green-colored bikes that are common near Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics (NUAA).

Features:

  • Discounts for students – Special promotions for university ID holders.
  • Lightweight design – Easy to maneuver in busy areas.
  • 24/7 availability – Ideal for late-night study sessions.

Best Routes Nearby:

  • Ming Xiaoling Mausoleum – A historical ride through lush greenery.
  • Presidential Palace to Xinjiekou – Explore Nanjing’s urban core.

4. Local Bike Shops – Best for Long-Term Rentals

If you're staying for a semester or longer, renting from a local shop might be more economical. Some recommended spots near Nanjing Agricultural University (NAU):

Nanjing Cycling Hub

  • Offers mountain bikes, road bikes, and e-bikes.
  • Monthly rentals start at ¥200.
  • Free maintenance included.

Campus Bike Rentals

Many universities have unofficial rental services run by students. Ask around for the best deals!

Safety Tips for Biking in Nanjing

Before hopping on a bike, keep these tips in mind:
- Always wear a helmet (though not common, it’s safer).
- Follow traffic rules – Stick to bike lanes where available.
- Lock your bike securely – Theft can happen in busy areas.
- Check the bike condition – Ensure brakes and tires are functional.

Hidden Gems to Explore by Bike

1. Purple Mountain (紫金山)

A must-visit for nature lovers. Rent an e-bike if the climb seems daunting.

2. Lao Men Dong (老门东)

A charming historic district with traditional architecture—perfect for a slow-paced ride.

3. Yangtze River Riverside Park

A long, peaceful route with stunning river views.

Final Thoughts

Whether you're a student looking for a daily commute solution or a traveler wanting to see Nanjing from a different perspective, bike rentals near universities offer flexibility and fun. With multiple options like HelloBike, Mobike, and local shops, you’ll find the perfect ride for your needs. So grab a bike, hit the road, and discover Nanjing’s beauty on two wheels!
