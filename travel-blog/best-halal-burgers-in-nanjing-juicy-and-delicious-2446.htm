Best Halal Burgers in Nanjing: Juicy and Delicious

  • Home
  • Travel Blog
  • Blog Details

Nanjing, a city steeped in history and culture, is also a hidden gem for food lovers. While the city is famous for its duck blood vermicelli soup and salted duck, there’s a rising star in its culinary scene: Halal burgers. Yes, you read that right—juicy, flavorful, and 100% Halal-certified burgers are making waves in Nanjing. Whether you're a Muslim traveler, a foodie exploring local flavors, or just someone craving a delicious burger, this guide will take you through the best spots to sink your teeth into Nanjing’s finest Halal burgers.

Why Halal Burgers in Nanjing?

Nanjing has a vibrant Muslim community, particularly around the Niujie (Ox Street) area, where Halal food has been a staple for centuries. With globalization and evolving tastes, Halal burgers have emerged as a fusion of traditional Islamic dietary laws and modern fast-food culture. These burgers aren’t just about compliance with Halal standards—they’re about bold flavors, high-quality ingredients, and unforgettable dining experiences.

Top 5 Halal Burger Joints in Nanjing

1. The Sultan’s Bite

Location: Near Confucius Temple

If you’re looking for a burger that packs a punch, The Sultan’s Bite is the place to be. Their signature Lamb & Beef Fusion Burger is a masterpiece—succulent minced lamb and beef patty, topped with caramelized onions, fresh lettuce, and a tangy yogurt-based sauce. The meat is sourced from local Halal butchers, ensuring both quality and authenticity.

Must-Try:

  • Spicy Harissa Burger (for those who love heat)
  • Truffle Fries (perfect sidekick)

2. Halal Grill House

Location: Xinjiekou District

A favorite among locals and expats alike, Halal Grill House offers a no-frills, meat-centric menu. Their Double Cheese Smash Burger is legendary—two thin, crispy-edged beef patties loaded with melted cheddar and a special house sauce. The buns are freshly baked daily, adding a soft yet sturdy texture to each bite.

Pro Tip:

Pair your burger with their Mint Lemonade for a refreshing contrast to the rich flavors.

3. Desert Oasis Burger Co.

Location: Near Zhongshan Gate

This spot brings Middle Eastern flair to Nanjing’s burger scene. The Shawarma Burger is a must-try—slow-roasted chicken or beef shawarma stuffed into a toasted bun with garlic sauce, pickles, and fries inside the burger (yes, you read that right). It’s messy, indulgent, and utterly delicious.

Unique Twist:

  • Date Milkshake (a sweet, creamy companion to your meal)

4. Nanjing Halal Express

Location: Close to Nanjing Railway Station

For travelers on the go, Nanjing Halal Express is a lifesaver. Their Classic Beef Burger is simple yet satisfying, featuring a juicy patty, crisp veggies, and a smoky BBQ sauce. What sets them apart? Speed and consistency—perfect for a quick bite before catching a train.

Traveler’s Pick:

  • Combo Meal (burger, fries, and a drink for under ¥50)

5. Spice Route Burgers

Location: Gulou District

If you love bold, aromatic flavors, Spice Route Burgers is your spot. Their Moroccan Spiced Lamb Burger is a flavor bomb—ground lamb mixed with cumin, coriander, and a hint of cinnamon, topped with roasted red peppers and feta cheese. Every bite transports you to the streets of Marrakech.

Don’t Miss:

  • Za’atar Dusted Fries (herby, addictive, and perfect for sharing)

What Makes These Burgers Special?

Quality Halal Meat

All these joints source their meat from certified Halal suppliers, ensuring ethical and high-standard preparation. The beef and lamb are always fresh, never frozen, which makes a huge difference in taste and texture.

Creative Fusion Flavors

From Middle Eastern spices to smoky American-style BBQ, these burgers aren’t just copies of Western fast food—they’re innovative fusions that respect Halal traditions while pushing culinary boundaries.

Affordable Luxury

Unlike high-end burger chains, these spots offer gourmet-quality burgers at reasonable prices, making them accessible to everyone.

Pairing Your Burger with Nanjing’s Sights

Why not turn your burger hunt into a mini food tour? Here’s how:
- Start at Confucius Temple, grab a burger at The Sultan’s Bite, then stroll along the Qinhuai River.
- After exploring Xinjiekou’s shopping district, refuel at Halal Grill House.
- Visit Zhongshan Gate for history, then head to Desert Oasis Burger Co. for a post-sightseeing feast.

Final Bite

Nanjing’s Halal burger scene is a testament to the city’s evolving food culture—rooted in tradition but embracing global influences. Whether you’re a local or a traveler, these juicy, delicious burgers are worth every bite. So next time you’re in Nanjing, skip the usual suspects and dive into these Halal-certified delights. Your taste buds will thank you.
