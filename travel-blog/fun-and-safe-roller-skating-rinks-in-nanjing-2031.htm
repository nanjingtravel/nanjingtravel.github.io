Fun and Safe Roller Skating Rinks in Nanjing

  • Home
  • Travel Blog
  • Blog Details

Roller skating is making a huge comeback worldwide, and Nanjing is no exception. Whether you're a seasoned skater or a beginner looking for some weekend fun, the city offers several fantastic roller skating rinks that combine excitement with safety. From indoor arenas with neon lights to outdoor spots with scenic views, Nanjing has something for everyone. Let’s dive into the best roller skating rinks in the city and why they’re worth a visit.

Top Roller Skating Rinks in Nanjing

1. Xuanwu Lake Skate Park

Located near the picturesque Xuanwu Lake, this outdoor skating spot is perfect for those who love fresh air and stunning views. The smooth pavement and spacious layout make it ideal for both casual skaters and those practicing tricks.

  • Why Visit?

    • Scenic lakeside setting
    • Free entry (bring your own skates or rent nearby)
    • Great for sunset skating sessions

  • Safety Tips:

    • Wear protective gear, especially if trying tricks.
    • Avoid crowded weekends for a smoother experience.

2. Joy City Indoor Skating Rink

If you prefer an air-conditioned environment with vibrant music and disco lights, Joy City’s rink is the place to be. This family-friendly spot is popular among teens and young adults.

  • Highlights:

    • Themed skate nights (e.g., disco, retro)
    • Skate rentals and beginner lessons available
    • Arcade games and food stalls nearby

  • Pro Tip:

    • Weekday afternoons are less crowded.

3. Deji Plaza Roller Rink

For a more upscale experience, head to Deji Plaza. This rink is known for its polished floors and stylish ambiance, attracting both locals and tourists.

  • What Makes It Special?

    • High-quality rental skates (inline and quad options)
    • Regular roller derby events
    • Close to shopping and dining

  • Safety First:

    • Staff frequently check equipment for wear and tear.
    • Helmets are mandatory for kids under 12.

Why Roller Skating Is a Must-Try in Nanjing

A Unique Way to Explore the City

Roller skating isn’t just confined to rinks—Nanjing’s smooth bike paths and riverside trails are perfect for urban skating. Join local skating groups for guided tours around Qinhuai River or the city’s historic streets.

Fitness and Fun Combined

Forget the gym! Skating is a full-body workout that improves balance and endurance. Many rinks in Nanjing also offer fitness-focused sessions with instructors.

Social Vibes

From weekend meetups to themed skate parties, Nanjing’s roller skating scene is incredibly social. It’s a great way to meet locals and fellow travelers.

Tips for First-Time Skaters in Nanjing

  • Rent Before You Buy – Most rinks offer affordable rentals, so test different skate styles before investing.
  • Dress Comfortably – Avoid loose clothing that could get caught in wheels.
  • Learn Basic Braking – Practice stopping techniques to avoid tumbles.
  • Check Event Schedules – Some rinks host beginner-friendly workshops.

Final Thoughts

Nanjing’s roller skating culture blends tradition with modern trends, offering something for every skill level. Whether you’re gliding under disco lights or cruising by Xuanwu Lake, the city’s rinks promise unforgettable memories. So lace up those skates and join the fun!
