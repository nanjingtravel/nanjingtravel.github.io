Nanjing’s Airport Shuttle Buses: Routes and Tips

  • Home
  • Travel Blog
  • Blog Details

Nanjing Lukou International Airport (NKG) is one of China’s busiest hubs, serving millions of travelers annually. While taxis and ride-hailing apps are popular, the airport shuttle buses remain a budget-friendly and efficient way to reach the city center or nearby destinations. Whether you’re a first-time visitor or a frequent flyer, here’s everything you need to know about Nanjing’s airport shuttle buses, including routes, schedules, and insider tips.

Why Choose Nanjing’s Airport Shuttle Buses?

Affordability

Compared to taxis or private transfers, shuttle buses are significantly cheaper. A one-way ticket typically costs between ¥20-¥40 (around $3-$6), making it ideal for solo travelers or groups on a budget.

Convenience

With multiple routes covering key areas like Nanjing Railway Station, Zhongyangmen, and even neighboring cities like Zhenjiang, the shuttle system eliminates the stress of navigating public transport with luggage.

Frequency

Buses run frequently, especially during peak hours, with intervals as short as 20-30 minutes. Late-night arrivals? Don’t worry—some routes operate until the last flight lands.

Major Shuttle Bus Routes

Route 1: Nanjing Railway Station

This is the most popular route, connecting the airport to Nanjing Railway Station in about 1 hour. From there, you can easily transfer to metro lines 1 or 3.
- Stops: None (direct service)
- Operating Hours: 6:00 AM – 11:00 PM
- Ticket Price: ¥20

Route 2: Zhongyangmen (Downtown Nanjing)

Perfect for travelers heading to hotels or attractions near Xinjiekou or Confucius Temple.
- Stops: Nanjing Railway Station, Zhongyangmen Bus Station
- Operating Hours: 7:00 AM – 10:30 PM
- Ticket Price: ¥25

Route 3: Jiangning University Town

A lifeline for students or visitors to universities like Southeast University or Nanjing Aeronautics and Astronautics University.
- Stops: Multiple campuses along Tongji Street
- Operating Hours: 8:00 AM – 9:00 PM
- Ticket Price: ¥30

Route 4: Zhenjiang (Intercity Service)

For those venturing beyond Nanjing, this route connects to Zhenjiang in about 1.5 hours.
- Stops: Zhenjiang Railway Station
- Operating Hours: 9:00 AM – 8:00 PM
- Ticket Price: ¥40

Pro Tips for a Smooth Ride

1. Buy Tickets in Advance

Tickets can be purchased at the airport’s shuttle bus counters (near Arrivals) or via WeChat/Alipay at self-service kiosks. Avoid long lines by buying online if possible.

2. Check Real-Time Updates

Schedules may adjust due to weather or traffic. Follow the official WeChat account “南京禄口机场快线” (Nanjing Lukou Airport Express) for live updates.

3. Luggage Rules

Each passenger is allowed one large suitcase and a carry-on. Oversized items (e.g., bikes) may incur extra fees.

4. Avoid Rush Hour

Buses get crowded during holidays or weekday peaks (7:00–9:00 AM, 5:00–7:00 PM). Arrive early to secure a seat.

5. Combine with Metro

For ultimate flexibility, take the shuttle to Nanjing Railway Station and switch to the metro (Line 3 goes straight to downtown).

Hidden Gems Along the Routes

For Foodies: Zhongyangmen Stop

Hop off here to explore Nanjing’s famous salted duck (盐水鸭) at Laomendong or snack on tangbao (soup dumplings) at Guobaoxiang.

For History Buffs: Confucius Temple Area

From the Zhongyangmen route, it’s a short taxi ride to this 1,000-year-old cultural hotspot. Don’t miss the Qinhuai River night cruise.

For Shoppers: Xinjiekou

Alight at Nanjing Railway Station and take Metro Line 1 to Xinjiekou, home to mega-malls like Deji Plaza.

Alternative Options

Taxis

Metered taxis cost ¥100–¥150 to downtown but are faster (30–40 minutes). Use Didi (China’s Uber) for fixed-price rides.

Metro

Line S1 connects the airport to Nanjing South Railway Station (transfer to Lines 1/3). Runs from 6:00 AM–10:00 PM.

Private Transfers

Hotels or Klook offer door-to-door services (~¥200). Ideal for families or late-night arrivals.

Final Notes

Nanjing’s shuttle buses strike a balance between cost and convenience. Whether you’re here for the history, food, or business, mastering this system will save you time and money. Pack your Suitcase, grab your ticket, and enjoy the ride into this dynamic city!
