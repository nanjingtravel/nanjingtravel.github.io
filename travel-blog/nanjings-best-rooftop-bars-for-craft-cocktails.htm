Nanjing’s Best Rooftop Bars for Craft Cocktails

  • Home
  • Travel Blog
  • Blog Details

Nanjing, a city steeped in history and modernity, offers a vibrant nightlife scene that’s best experienced from above. Rooftop bars here combine stunning skyline views with expertly crafted cocktails, making them a must-visit for travelers and locals alike. Whether you’re sipping on a gin-infused creation or a locally inspired mix, these elevated spots deliver unforgettable moments. Here’s your guide to the best rooftop bars in Nanjing for craft cocktails.

The Skyline Sippers: Top Picks for Elevated Drinking

1. Flair at The Ritz-Carlton, Nanjing

Perched on the 62nd floor, Flair is the highest rooftop bar in Nanjing, offering panoramic views of the Yangtze River and the city’s glittering skyline. The bar’s mixologists are artists, blending premium spirits with exotic ingredients. Try their signature Nanjing Mule, a twist on the classic Moscow Mule, featuring local ginger beer and a hint of osmanthus syrup. The sleek, contemporary design and live DJ sets make it a hotspot for cosmopolitan crowds.

2. Xuanwu Lounge at The Fairmont Nanjing

Overlooking the serene Xuanwu Lake, this rooftop bar is a sanctuary of sophistication. The cocktail menu is a fusion of East-meets-West, with highlights like the Jinling Sour—a bold mix of baijiu, lemon, and egg white. The lounge’s open-air terrace is ideal for sunset cocktails, and their curated selection of small plates pairs perfectly with their inventive drinks.

3. Cloud 9 at InterContinental Nanjing

True to its name, Cloud 9 feels like a slice of heaven. Located on the 78th floor, it’s one of the highest bars in Asia. The Purple Mountain Martini, made with Nanjing’s famous purple clay-infused vodka, is a must-try. The bar’s modern Chinese aesthetic, complete with soft lighting and plush seating, creates an intimate vibe perfect for date nights or solo indulgence.

Hidden Gems: Off-the-Beaten-Path Rooftop Bars

4. The Secret Garden at Nanjing Green Tea Hotel

Tucked away in a boutique hotel, this rooftop bar is a lush escape from the urban hustle. Their craft cocktails feature tea-infused spirits, paying homage to Nanjing’s tea culture. The Dragon Well Gimlet, combining gin with Longjing tea and lime, is a refreshing standout. The bar’s bohemian décor and string lights add a whimsical touch.

5. Bamboo House Rooftop

A lesser-known gem in the Xinjiekou district, Bamboo House offers a cozy, intimate setting. Their mixologist specializes in bespoke cocktails—just describe your mood, and they’ll whip up something unique. The Bamboo Old Fashioned, infused with bamboo charcoal and aged whiskey, is a smoky delight. The bar’s rustic charm and friendly vibe make it a local favorite.

Cocktails with a View: What to Order

Nanjing-Inspired Craft Cocktails

  • Qinhuai River Breeze: A light, floral cocktail with gin, elderflower liqueur, and a splash of local lotus root syrup.
  • Ming Dynasty Mule: A spicy-sweet blend of rum, ginger, and chrysanthemum honey, served in a copper mug.
  • Stone City Smash: Bourbon muddled with fresh peaches and a dash of black vinegar for a tangy kick.

Global Favorites with a Twist

  • Yuzu Margarita: Tequila meets Nanjing’s beloved yuzu citrus for a zesty, refreshing sip.
  • Sichuan Negroni: A fiery take on the classic, infused with Sichuan peppercorns for a numbing finish.

Tips for Visiting Nanjing’s Rooftop Bars

  • Timing is Key: Arrive just before sunset to snag the best seats and watch the city transition from day to night.
  • Dress to Impress: Most upscale rooftop bars enforce a smart-casual dress code.
  • Reservations Recommended: Popular spots like Flair and Cloud 9 often require advance booking, especially on weekends.
  • Explore Beyond Cocktails: Many bars offer tapas or full menus—pair your drinks with local delicacies like salted duck or sesame cakes.

Nanjing’s rooftop bars are more than just drinking spots—they’re experiences that blend mixology, culture, and breathtaking views. Whether you’re a cocktail connoisseur or a traveler seeking the city’s pulse, these elevated venues promise nights to remember.
