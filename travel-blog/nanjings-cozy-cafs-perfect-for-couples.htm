Nanjing’s Cozy Cafés Perfect for Couples

  • Home
  • Travel Blog
  • Blog Details

Nanjing, a city steeped in history and culture, is also home to a thriving café scene that offers intimate spaces for couples to unwind. Whether you’re looking for a quiet corner to share a cup of coffee or a charming spot with Instagram-worthy décor, Nanjing’s cafés deliver romance in every sip. Here’s a curated list of the coziest cafés perfect for lovebirds exploring the city.

1. The Vintage Charm: Lost in Time Café

A Nostalgic Escape

Tucked away in a quiet alley near Confucius Temple, Lost in Time Café feels like stepping into a bygone era. The café’s interior is adorned with antique typewriters, vintage vinyl records, and sepia-toned photographs, creating a warm, nostalgic atmosphere.

Must-Try Drinks

  • Rose Latte: A fragrant blend of espresso and rose syrup, topped with edible petals.
  • Mocha Affogato: A decadent mix of espresso poured over vanilla ice cream.

Couples can cozy up on plush velvet couches or sit by the large bay window, watching the world go by. The soft jazz playlist adds to the intimate vibe, making it a perfect spot for deep conversations.

2. The Garden Oasis: Florist’s Secret

A Blooming Paradise

Located near Xuanwu Lake, Florist’s Secret is a café-florist hybrid where every table is surrounded by fresh flowers. The glass ceiling allows natural light to flood the space, creating a dreamy, sunlit ambiance.

Romantic Picks

  • Lavender Honey Tea: A soothing herbal infusion with a touch of sweetness.
  • Strawberry Cheesecake: Light, creamy, and topped with fresh berries.

The café also offers flower-arranging workshops—perfect for couples who want to create something beautiful together.

3. The Book Lover’s Hideaway: Pages & Beans

Literary Love

For couples who bond over books, Pages & Beans is a dream come true. This café, nestled in the Gulou district, features floor-to-ceiling bookshelves stocked with both Chinese and English titles.

Cozy Nooks

  • The Writer’s Corner: A secluded booth with a vintage desk and a leather-bound journal for guests to leave notes.
  • The Window Seat: Overlooking a quiet courtyard, ideal for shared reading sessions.

Try their Salted Caramel Cappuccino or Matcha Tiramisu while flipping through a novel together.

4. The Minimalist Retreat: Cloud Nine Café

Serenity in Simplicity

If modern aesthetics appeal to you, Cloud Nine Café in Xinjiekou offers a sleek, minimalist design with soft neutral tones and clean lines. The open layout and floor-to-ceiling windows create an airy, relaxed vibe.

Signature Sips

  • Cold Brew with Orange Zest: A refreshing twist on classic cold brew.
  • White Chocolate Raspberry Mousse: A delicate balance of sweet and tart.

The café’s quiet ambiance makes it ideal for couples who prefer a more understated, sophisticated setting.

5. The Rooftop Romance: Skyline Brews

Views & Vibes

Perched atop a building in the bustling 1912 district, Skyline Brews offers panoramic views of Nanjing’s skyline. As the sun sets, the café transforms into a magical spot with fairy lights and candlelit tables.

Evening Specials

  • Espresso Martini: A caffeinated cocktail for a little spark.
  • Chocolate Fondue for Two: Dip fresh fruit and marshmallows into rich, melted chocolate.

This is the ultimate date-night destination, especially if you’re celebrating a special occasion.

6. The Artistic Haven: Palette & Pour

Creativity Meets Coffee

For couples who appreciate art, Palette & Pour in the Nanjing Art District is a must-visit. The walls are covered in rotating local artwork, and the café even hosts live painting sessions.

Art-Inspired Menu

  • Van Gogh Latte: A vibrant turmeric-and-matcha swirl.
  • Picasso Pancakes: Colorful stacks with berry compote.

You can sip coffee while sketching each other—or just admire the creativity around you.

7. The Hidden Gem: Alley Cat Café

Quirky & Intimate

True to its name, Alley Cat Café is a tiny, hard-to-find spot in an old hutong. The café is filled with feline-themed décor, and a resident cat often curls up next to guests.

Cozy Comforts

  • Spiced Chai Latte: Warm and aromatic.
  • Homemade Apple Crumble: Served with vanilla bean ice cream.

It’s the kind of place where time slows down, making it perfect for lazy afternoon dates.

Final Thoughts

Nanjing’s café scene offers endless possibilities for couples, whether you’re into vintage charm, floral beauty, or urban skyline views. Each of these spots brings something unique to the table—literally and figuratively. So grab your partner, pick a café, and let Nanjing’s cozy corners set the mood for romance.
