Nanjing’s Top Family-Friendly Hotels

  • Home
  • Travel Blog
  • Blog Details

Nanjing, a city steeped in history and modern charm, is a fantastic destination for families. Whether you’re exploring the ancient city walls, wandering through the lush Zhongshan Mountain Scenic Area, or indulging in local delicacies like salted duck, having the right hotel can make all the difference. Here’s a curated list of Nanjing’s best family-friendly hotels that cater to both parents and kids, ensuring a memorable stay.

Luxury Stays for the Whole Family

The Ritz-Carlton, Nanjing

Located in the heart of Xinjiekou, The Ritz-Carlton offers unparalleled luxury with family-friendly amenities. Spacious suites provide extra room for kids to play, while the hotel’s babysitting services allow parents to enjoy a quiet dinner at their Michelin-starred restaurant. The indoor pool is a hit with children, and the concierge can arrange kid-friendly city tours.

Fairmont Nanjing

Overlooking the Yangtze River, Fairmont Nanjing combines elegance with practicality for families. Their "Fairmont Kids" program includes welcome gifts, child-sized bathrobes, and even cooking classes for little chefs. The hotel’s proximity to Hongshan Forest Zoo makes it a prime choice for animal-loving families.

Mid-Range Gems with Kid-Centric Perks

Holiday Inn Nanjing Aqua City

Situated next to the bustling Aqua City shopping complex, this hotel is perfect for families who love convenience. The "Kids Stay & Eat Free" policy is a budget-saver, and the indoor play area keeps children entertained. Parents will appreciate the easy access to Confucius Temple, just a short walk away.

Novotel Nanjing Central

Novotel’s reputation for family-friendly stays shines here. The hotel features a dedicated kids’ club with supervised activities, so parents can relax at the spa. Rooms are designed with bunk beds and gaming consoles, making it a dream for older children. Don’t miss their weekend family brunch buffet!

Budget-Friendly Options Without Compromising Fun

Jinling Hotel Nanjing

A historic landmark with modern comforts, Jinling Hotel offers interconnecting rooms ideal for larger families. The lush garden courtyards provide a safe space for kids to run around, and the hotel’s location near Nanjing Museum means cultural outings are effortless.

GreenTree Inn Nanjing Confucius Temple

For families watching their wallets, GreenTree Inn delivers clean, cozy rooms and a prime location. The nearby Qinhuai River night market is a hit with teens, while younger kids will love the hotel’s board game library.

Unique Stays for Adventurous Families

Nanjing Yijia Hot Spring Resort

If your family enjoys nature, this resort nestled in Tangshan Hot Springs is a must. Private villas with hot spring baths offer relaxation, while the on-site farm lets kids pick fresh fruit. It’s a short drive to Yangshan Quarry Park, where hiking trails abound.

Camping at Purple Mountain

For a non-traditional experience, try glamping near Purple Mountain. Several family-friendly campsites offer pre-set tents, BBQ facilities, and stargazing activities. It’s a great way to bond while staying close to the city’s top attractions.

Tips for Booking Family-Friendly Hotels in Nanjing

  • Check for cribs and extra beds: Many hotels offer these for free or a small fee.
  • Look for proximity to parks or attractions: Less transit time means happier kids.
  • Inquire about meal plans: Buffets with kid-friendly options save time and stress.
  • Read reviews: Other parents’ experiences can highlight hidden perks or pitfalls.

Nanjing’s blend of history, nature, and vibrant city life makes it a joy for families. With these hotel picks, you’re guaranteed a stay that’s as relaxing as it is exciting. Happy travels!
