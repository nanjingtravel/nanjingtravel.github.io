The Best Camping Spots Near Purple Mountain

  • Home
  • Travel Blog
  • Blog Details

Purple Mountain, or Zǐjīn Shān (紫金山), is one of Nanjing’s most iconic landmarks, renowned for its lush forests, historical sites, and breathtaking views. While hiking and sightseeing are popular activities, camping near Purple Mountain offers a unique way to immerse yourself in nature. Whether you’re a seasoned camper or a first-timer, these spots provide the perfect blend of tranquility and adventure.

Why Camp Near Purple Mountain?

Camping near Purple Mountain isn’t just about pitching a tent—it’s about experiencing Nanjing’s natural beauty up close. The area boasts:

  • Starry Night Skies: Away from city lights, the night sky here is perfect for stargazing.
  • Rich Biodiversity: The mountain is home to diverse flora and fauna, making it a haven for nature enthusiasts.
  • Proximity to Attractions: Many campsites are close to historical sites like the Ming Xiaoling Mausoleum and Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum.

Top 5 Camping Spots Near Purple Mountain

1. Zhongshan Botanical Garden Campsite

A Botanical Paradise

Nestled at the foot of Purple Mountain, this campsite is ideal for families and solo travelers alike. The botanical garden’s vast greenery provides a serene backdrop, and campers can explore themed gardens during the day.

Highlights:
- Easy access to hiking trails leading up Purple Mountain.
- Safe and well-maintained facilities, including clean restrooms.
- Nighttime guided tours to spot fireflies in summer.

2. Baima Lake (White Horse Lake) Campsite

Lakeside Serenity

About 30 minutes from Purple Mountain, Baima Lake offers a peaceful retreat with crystal-clear waters. It’s a favorite for kayaking, fishing, and lakeside bonfires.

Highlights:
- Spacious lakeside plots perfect for group camping.
- Sunrise views over the water are unforgettable.
- Nearby farm stays offer fresh, local produce for campfire meals.

3. Purple Mountain Backcountry Camping Zone

For the Adventurous Souls

This unofficial camping area is for those who prefer solitude. Located on the quieter western slopes of Purple Mountain, it’s a great spot for backpackers.

Tips:
- Bring your own water and supplies—no facilities here.
- Permits may be required; check with local authorities.
- Ideal for autumn camping when the foliage turns golden.

4. Xuanwu Lake Lakeside Campground

Urban Camping with a View

While not directly on Purple Mountain, Xuanwu Lake’s campground offers stunning views of the mountain skyline. It’s perfect for those who want a mix of nature and city convenience.

Highlights:
- Rentable equipment, so you don’t need to bring your own.
- Nighttime paddle boating under the stars.
- Walking distance to Nanjing’s vibrant food streets.

5. Tangshan Hot Springs Campsite

Camping with a Luxe Twist

A short drive from Purple Mountain, this campsite combines rugged camping with relaxing hot springs. After a day of hiking, soak in natural geothermal pools.

Highlights:
- Private camping plots with optional hot spring access.
- Family-friendly with BBQ pits and picnic areas.
- Stunning views of the surrounding hills.

Essential Camping Tips for Purple Mountain

Packing Checklist

  • Tent & Sleeping Gear: Nights can get chilly, even in summer.
  • Bug Repellent: Mosquitoes are active near water sources.
  • Local Snacks: Try Nanjing’s salted duck or sesame cakes for a campfire treat.

Best Times to Camp

  • Spring (March-May): Mild weather and blooming flowers.
  • Autumn (September-November): Cool temperatures and vibrant foliage.

Leave No Trace

Respect the environment by:
- Packing out all trash.
- Avoiding loud noises to preserve the tranquility.
- Using designated fire pits.

Beyond Camping: Nearby Activities

  • Hiking Purple Mountain: The sunrise from the peak is a must-see.
  • Exploring Ming Xiaoling Mausoleum: A UNESCO World Heritage Site.
  • Biking Around Xuanwu Lake: Rent a bike and enjoy the scenic loop.

Whether you’re seeking adventure or relaxation, camping near Purple Mountain offers an unforgettable escape into nature. Pack your gear, gather your friends, and get ready to experience Nanjing’s wild side!
