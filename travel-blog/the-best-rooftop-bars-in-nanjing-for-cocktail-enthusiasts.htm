The Best Rooftop Bars in Nanjing for Cocktail Enthusiasts

  • Home
  • Travel Blog
  • Blog Details

Nanjing, a city steeped in history and modernity, offers a vibrant nightlife scene that’s perfect for cocktail lovers. Whether you’re a local or a traveler, sipping a crafted cocktail while soaking in panoramic views of the city is an experience not to be missed. Here’s a curated list of the best rooftop bars in Nanjing that promise unforgettable evenings.

1. Flair Rooftop Bar at The Ritz-Carlton

Elevated Luxury with a View

Perched on the 62nd floor of The Ritz-Carlton, Flair Rooftop Bar is the epitome of sophistication. The bar boasts a sleek, contemporary design with floor-to-ceiling windows offering breathtaking views of the Yangtze River and the city skyline.

Signature Cocktails to Try

  • Nanjing Mule: A local twist on the Moscow Mule, featuring Jiangsu-inspired spices.
  • Purple Mountain Sour: A refreshing blend of gin, purple yam, and citrus, paying homage to Nanjing’s iconic Purple Mountain.

Why It’s a Must-Visit

Flair’s impeccable service and live DJ sets make it a hotspot for both tourists and Nanjing’s elite. The bar’s outdoor terrace is perfect for warm evenings, offering an unbeatable ambiance.

2. Sky 52 at InterContinental

A Sky-High Oasis

Located on the 52nd floor of the InterContinental, Sky 52 is one of the highest rooftop bars in Nanjing. The modern, minimalist decor complements the stunning 360-degree views, making it a favorite for sunset cocktails.

Must-Try Drinks

  • Qinhuai Old Fashioned: A smoky, rich cocktail inspired by the historic Qinhuai River.
  • Jinling Fizz: A light, effervescent drink with local lychee and prosecco.

The Vibe

Sky 52’s relaxed yet upscale atmosphere is ideal for unwinding after a day of exploring Nanjing’s cultural landmarks. The bar also offers a selection of tapas-style bites to pair with your drinks.

3. The Roof at The Fairmont

Chic and Trendy

The Roof at The Fairmont is a stylish rooftop bar known for its trendy crowd and creative mixology. The bar features a mix of indoor and outdoor seating, with cozy fire pits for cooler evenings.

Cocktail Highlights

  • Ming Dynasty Martini: A bold fusion of baijiu and vermouth, garnished with a candied lotus root.
  • Sunset Over Xuanwu: A tropical-inspired cocktail with rum, passionfruit, and a hint of chili for a kick.

Why You’ll Love It

The Roof frequently hosts themed nights and guest DJs, making it a dynamic spot for nightlife enthusiasts. The bar’s Instagram-worthy decor and inventive drinks are a big draw.

4. Cloud 9 at The Grand Mansion

Vintage Glamour Meets Modern Mixology

Cloud 9 offers a unique blend of old-world charm and contemporary flair. The bar’s Art Deco-inspired interiors and plush seating create a luxurious setting for cocktail aficionados.

Standout Sips

  • Nanjing Negroni: A twist on the classic, infused with osmanthus for a floral note.
  • The Scholar’s Cup: A whiskey-based cocktail with chrysanthemum honey, inspired by Nanjing’s literary history.

The Experience

Cloud 9’s intimate setting and live jazz performances make it perfect for a romantic evening or a relaxed night out with friends. The bartenders are known for their personalized cocktail recommendations.

5. Vantage Lounge at The Waldorf Astoria

Unparalleled Elegance

Vantage Lounge is synonymous with luxury. The bar’s sophisticated ambiance, coupled with its prime location overlooking the Confucius Temple, makes it a standout destination.

Signature Creations

  • Golden Phoenix: A sparkling cocktail with gin, elderflower, and gold leaf, symbolizing Nanjing’s imperial past.
  • Porcelain Sour: A visually stunning drink served in a hand-painted porcelain cup, featuring vodka and plum wine.

What Sets It Apart

Vantage Lounge’s attention to detail—from the bespoke glassware to the curated playlist—ensures a memorable experience. The bar also offers a premium selection of cigars for those who enjoy a smoky finish to their evening.

6. The Nest at The Hyatt

A Lively Social Hub

The Nest is a favorite among young professionals and travelers looking for a vibrant atmosphere. The bar’s industrial-chic design and energetic vibe make it a great place to mingle.

Top Picks

  • Spiced Dragon: A fiery mix of tequila, dragon fruit, and Sichuan peppercorns.
  • Nanjing Espresso Martini: A caffeinated kick with a local twist—infused with lotus seed syrup.

The Crowd

The Nest’s happy hour deals and regular live music events attract a fun, diverse crowd. It’s the perfect spot to start your night before hitting Nanjing’s bustling club scene.

7. The Terrace at The Shangri-La

Relaxed Sophistication

The Terrace offers a more laid-back rooftop experience, with lush greenery and comfortable lounge seating. The bar’s serene ambiance is ideal for those looking to escape the city’s hustle.

Drink Specialties

  • Bamboo Grove: A light, herbaceous cocktail with gin, bamboo shoot syrup, and mint.
  • Yangtze Breeze: A refreshing mix of vodka, cucumber, and lime, perfect for warm nights.

Why Visit?

The Terrace’s focus on fresh, local ingredients and its tranquil setting make it a hidden gem. It’s a great place to unwind with a book or enjoy a quiet conversation.

8. The Aviary at The St. Regis

Avant-Garde Mixology

The Aviary is for those who appreciate experimental cocktails. The bar’s innovative techniques and theatrical presentations set it apart from the rest.

Must-Experience Drinks

  • Smoke & Mirrors: A mezcal-based cocktail served under a glass cloche filled with aromatic smoke.
  • The Forbidden City: A visually striking drink with layers of colors, inspired by Nanjing’s historical sites.

The Wow Factor

The Aviary’s interactive cocktail menu and skilled mixologists make every visit a unique adventure. It’s a must for cocktail enthusiasts seeking something extraordinary.

9. The Lantern at The Mandarin Oriental

Intimate and Exquisite

The Lantern offers a cozy, lantern-lit setting with stunning views of the city. The bar’s warm, inviting atmosphere makes it a favorite for date nights.

Cocktail Gems

  • Lotus Dream: A delicate blend of sake, coconut, and lotus flower essence.
  • Midnight in Nanjing: A deep, complex cocktail with aged rum and black sesame.

The Appeal

The Lantern’s focus on Asian-inspired flavors and its serene ambiance create a truly special experience. The bar also offers a selection of artisanal teas for non-drinkers.

10. The Summit at The Four Seasons

Peak Perfection

The Summit is the ultimate destination for those who want to indulge in luxury. The bar’s opulent decor and unparalleled service ensure a night to remember.

Elite Elixirs

  • Imperial Jade: A gin-based cocktail with jade-colored herbal liqueur and kumquat.
  • The Four Seasons Sour: A seasonal rotating cocktail showcasing the best local produce.

The Final Word

The Summit’s exclusivity and attention to detail make it the crown jewel of Nanjing’s rooftop bar scene. It’s the perfect place to celebrate a special occasion or simply treat yourself.

Whether you’re a cocktail connoisseur or just looking for a stunning spot to enjoy a drink, Nanjing’s rooftop bars offer something for everyone. From sky-high luxury to laid-back lounges, these venues promise unforgettable nights under the stars.
